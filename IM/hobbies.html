Another hobby that I plough hours into everyday, is music. I tend to listen to listen to music extremely often when I'm travelling between university and home, as well as going back and forth between work. Genres of music I tend to listen to are "Indie pop" with the occasional tune from the 80s or late 90s.

The feature to the left is a small playlist, created by me, where by clicking the name of the song the song will play using the audio HTML 5 tag. Once the song completes, the next song will play, and if you reach the end of the playlist, the playlist will loop back to the top. Note: The songs may be quite loud, so I have set the default volume to 0.18 in order to preserve your hearing.
